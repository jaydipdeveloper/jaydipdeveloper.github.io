Job Description:

Here from Prodev, it seems perfect opportunity for you to join our team as per your skill and expertise. Team prodev looking for enthusiastic iOS developer to join our team.

Skills Required:

  • - Should have at least 1 to 2 years experience in developing mobile applications for iPhone/iPad
  • - Should have strong basics in Swift, Swift UI, Objective-C and related IOS Frameworks
  • - Should have a good understanding of Swift/Xcode and related tools
  • - Must have very strong experience with web services, Multithreading, and XML Parsing/JSON
  • - Familiar with IOS SDK
  • - Experience with third-party libraries and APIs
  • - Experience with performance and memory tuning with tools to build battery-optimized location-based app.
  • - Follow effective design and development practices on iOS
  • - Ensure the performance, quality, and responsiveness of applications.
  • - Write beautifully crafted code, easy to read, with a high level of technical organization.
  • - Knowledge of other web technologies and UI/UX standards.
  • - Comprehensive knowledge and experience of iOS SDK (UIKit, Cocoa Touch, Core Data, Core Location, etc.) using XCode.
